## üì± Telegram Bot Setup

Transform your AI assistant into a personal Telegram bot for on-the-go assistance!

### Step 1: Enable Telegram Integration

1. In AI Query Hub, go to **Settings ‚Üí Integrations**
2. Find **"Telegram Bot"** section
3. Click **"Connect Telegram"**
4. Copy the provided connection code

### Step 2: Link Your Telegram Account

1. Open Telegram and search for **@aiqueryhub_bot** (or use the provided bot name)
2. Start a chat with the bot
3. Send the command: `/start [your-connection-code]`
4. You'll receive confirmation that your account is linked

### Step 3: Using Telegram Commands

**Basic Commands:**
- `/help` - Show available commands
- `/status` - Check your account status
- `/settings` - Manage bot preferences

**Natural Conversation:**
- Simply type or speak your questions
- Send voice messages for transcription
- Share images for analysis
- Forward messages for AI assistance

**Advanced Features:**
- **Proactive Assistance**: Your bot can reach out proactively with reminders
- **Voice Recognition**: Send voice messages for hands-free interaction
- **Image Analysis**: Share screenshots or photos for AI insights
- **Document Queries**: Ask about your uploaded documents

### Privacy & Security

- Your Telegram messages are encrypted and secure
- Only you can access your AI assistant through your linked account
- You can revoke Telegram access anytime in Settings

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't log in"**
- Check your email for the confirmation link
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact support if issues persist

**"My documents won't upload"**
- Ensure file size is under 50MB
- Check that file format is supported
- Try uploading one file at a time
- Refresh the page and try again

**"Telegram bot isn't responding"**
- Verify your connection code was entered correctly
- Try unlinking and reconnecting your account
- Check that the bot isn't blocked in your Telegram settings
- Send `/start` command again to reinitialize

**"Slack integration not working"**
- Confirm the app was properly authorized in your workspace
- Check that you have permission to add apps
- Try removing and re-adding the integration
- Verify the app has necessary channel permissions

**"AI responses are slow"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Large document queries may take longer to process
- Consider using simpler queries for faster responses

**"Proactive check-ins not working"**
- Verify your preferred contact method is set correctly
- Check that notifications are enabled for your chosen platform
- Ensure your AI has sufficient context to provide assistance
- Review autonomy settings in your dashboard
