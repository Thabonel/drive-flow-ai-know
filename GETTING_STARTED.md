# Welcome to AI Query Hub!

Hi James! Here's a quick guide to get you started.

---

## What is AI Query Hub?

AI Query Hub is your personal AI assistant that remembers everything. Upload your documents, and the AI will help you search, summarize, and answer questions about your content.

---

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Sign In
Go to [aiqueryhub.com](https://aiqueryhub.com) and sign in with your email and password.

### Step 2: Upload Your First Document
1. Click **"Documents"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Documents"**
3. Upload a PDF, Word doc, or text file
4. Wait for the AI to process it (usually takes a few seconds)

### Step 3: Ask the AI
1. Click on **"AI Assistant"** in the sidebar (or the chat icon)
2. Type a question about your document
3. The AI will search your documents and give you an answer!

**Example questions:**
- "Summarize my document"
- "What are the key points?"
- "Find information about [topic]"

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific** - Instead of "tell me about the document", ask "what are the main recommendations in this report?"

2. **Upload relevant docs** - The AI works best when your documents are focused on topics you'll ask about

3. **Use Knowledge Bases** - Group related documents for better, more focused answers
